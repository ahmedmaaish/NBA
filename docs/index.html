How this works

📅 When games show up:

  • Regular season (Oct–Apr): 5–15 games per night, 20–40 signals fire daily
  • Playoffs (Apr–Jun): 1–4 games per night, fewer signals
  • NBA Finals (Jun): Only 2 teams playing — usually 1 game per matchup day
  • Off-season (Jun–Oct): No NBA games, dashboard will be empty

Strategies were tested against 85,464 games across 10 leagues (NBA, Euroleague, EuroCup, Spain ACB, Germany BBL, Turkey BSL, Italy LBA, France LNB, Israel BSL, Lithuania LKL). Signals fire only when a real mathematical edge is detected — most games show "no signal" which is correct behavior.

📊 Backtest accuracy & data sources

Two tiers of data quality:

  • API tier — high trust: NBA (Kaggle, 73k games), Euroleague (InCrowd API, 3k games), EuroCup (1.8k games). Exact dates, official source, quarter scores.
  • Wikipedia tier — medium trust: ACB, BBL, BSL (Turkey), LBA, LNB, Israeli BSL, LKL. Scores accurate but dates are approximated (spread evenly across season window).

✅ No-lookahead bias verified (50/50 random samples passed).
✅ Home court advantage 55–62% across all leagues (matches reality).
⚠ For Wikipedia-sourced leagues, rest-day strategies (S1, S7) have higher noise. Form/win-rate/season strategies (S5, S13, S14, S15, S16) work fully.
⚠ Treat win rates from wiki-tier leagues as ±5% accurate.

📖 22bet glossary

The app uses 22bet's exact terms in the betting instructions so they match what you see on the app.

What the app saysWhat 22bet calls itWhat it means
Team Wins Team Wins (2 options) The team to win, including overtime. The simplest market.
Handicap Handicap (34 lines) Point spread — pick a team to win by more than X points, or lose by less than X points.
Total Total (34 lines) Combined score over or under a line (e.g., 217.5). Both teams' points combined.
💡 On 22bet always tap the green "Including Overtime" tab at the top, then scroll to find these markets. Other markets like "1X2 In Regular Time", "Total 1", "Total 2", "Handicap 1 + Total" — the app's strategies don't use these.
